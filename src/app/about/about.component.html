About

Peter Parker is a former college student who was bitten by a genetically modified spider. The spider that bit him had extreme speed, stength, senses, jumping abilites, and combative nature. Parker then became like this spider. With his newly "inherited" powers and abilities, he could now climb up buildings, shoot extremely strong web and swing from it, and fight the most powerful criminals. With this, Peter Parker set out to save New York City from constant crime as we know it to have. He did this while keeping his spiderman identity anonymous and being in love with the same woman he's loved since childhood.
